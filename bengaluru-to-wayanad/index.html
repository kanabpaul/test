Wayanad

"വയലുകളുടെ നാട്" - The Land of Paddy Fields

Route Overview

From: Bengaluru, Karnataka
To: Wayanad, Kerala

Via: Nagarhole National Park (Mandya → Mysore → Bandipur National Park) → Vythiri, Kerala

Route 1 (Popular Scenic Drive)

  • Bangalore → Ramanagara → Channapatna → Srirangapatna → Mysore → Gundlupete → Muthanga → Wayanad

Toll Info

Tolls applicable along NH275 & NH766. Keep change handy and FASTag ready.

Food Stops

  • Kamat Lokaruchi, Ramanagara – Classic South Indian breakfast stop
  • Poojari Fish Land, Naganahalli – Great for seafood lovers
  • 1980’s A Nostalgic Restaurant, Kalpetta – Authentic Kerala cuisine in vintage vibes
  • Wilton Hotel & Restaurant, Sulthan Bathery – Delicious multi-cuisine with great ambiance

Top Attractions in Wayanad

  • Banasura Sagar Dam – India's largest earthen dam
  • Kuruvadweep – River island on the Kabini River
  • Edakkal Caves – Prehistoric rock engravings
  • Soochipara Waterfalls – Ideal for nature lovers and trekkers
  • Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary – Spot elephants and exotic birds
